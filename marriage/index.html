Strengthening Marriages

Part A from the manual "Marriage and Family Relations"

Summary

What is it?

The Marriage and Family Relations course is designed to help Church members strengthen marriages and families and find joy in their family relationships. This course covers 8 lessons over 6 weeks from Part A of the Marriage and Family Relations manual.

Who is it for?

All married couples or those who are preparing to get married.

Course Outline

  • Week 1
    • The Family is Central to the Creators Plan
  • Week 2
    • Developing Unity in Marriage
  • Week 3
    • Nurturing Love and Friendship in Marriage
  • Week 4
    • Responding to Challenges in Marriage
    • Responding to Challenges through Positive Communication
  • Week 5
    • Strengthening Marriage through Faith and Prayer
    • The Healing Power of Forgiveness
  • Week 6
    • Managing Family Finances
