Outline

What is it?

The Strengthening Marriage course is designed to help Church members strengthen marriages and families and find joy in their family relationships. The course is 6 sessions from the Strengthening Marriage manual.

Who is it for?

All married couples (young or old) or those who are preparing to get married.

Why during Sunday School time?

Sunday school time is preferred so that everyone can attend and you don't need to arrange baby sitters for a mid-week outing.

I'm in teaching another class

Let us know and we'll arrange to get a replacement teacher for your class.

I can't attend but I'm still interested

We understand that you may have a calling or other responsibility that prevents you from attending. We will update this site during the course. I might be able to record the lessons so you can listen to them later on but only if there is interest - so let me know.
